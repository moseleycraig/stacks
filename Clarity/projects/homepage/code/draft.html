Frequently Asked Questions

How does Bitcoin anchoring work?
Port Authority anchors each price update to Bitcoin blocks through a multi-step validation process. Oracle submissions must reference valid Bitcoin block hashes and wait for 6-block confirmations before finalization, ensuring mathematical certainty.
What makes this more secure than traditional oracles?
Traditional oracles rely on economic incentives that can be attacked. Port Authority leverages Bitcoin's hash power - the most secure network in the world - making attacks mathematically impossible at scale.
Which DeFi protocols integrate with Port Authority?
Currently integrated with ALEX Lab for secure AMM trading, Velar for leverage with mathematical guarantees, and Hermetica for Bitcoin-backed synthetic assets. More integrations coming soon.
How can I become an oracle operator?
Oracle operators must stake STX and BTC bonds, maintain high uptime, and pass our 8-layer validation system. Contact our team for operator requirements and onboarding process.
